## ROI Calculation (Year 1)

- **Total cost (dev + 1 year ops):** ₱112,500 + ₱15,600 = **₱128,100**
- **Total tangible benefits (Year 1):** **₱82,500**
- **Net benefit:** ₱82,500 – ₱128,100 = **–₱45,600** (negative)
- **ROI:** (Net benefit / Cost) × 100 = (–45,600 / 128,100) × 100 = **–35.6%**

## Recommendation

**Proceed with caution (revise plan).**

### Justification
- The project has a negative ROI in the first year primarily because development cost is high (even at ₱150/hour) and tangible benefits are modest.
- However, the **intangible benefits** (learning, portfolio, team building) are valuable for an academic setting.
- **To improve ROI:**
  - Use the free Render tier to keep ops cost near zero.
  - Open‑source the project to gain community feedback (no direct revenue).
  - If deployed in a corporate training environment, the time‑saving benefit could be much higher (e.g., ₱500/hour for senior developers).

## Case Study (Philippine context)

> **If this system is used by 20 remote teams (each 4–6 members) in a BPO or tech company for weekly team‑building sessions, and each session saves 30 minutes of manual coordination, the annual time saved would be:**  
> 20 teams × 1 hour/week × 50 weeks = 1,000 hours.  
> At an average team lead salary of ₱500/hour, that’s **₱500,000 in tangible benefits per year** – turning the ROI strongly positive (>150%).  
> For a classroom of 100 IT students, the learning value and engagement boost are even harder to quantify but clearly beneficial.

## Conclusion

SketchGuess is **not a profitable commercial product** in its current form, but it is an **excellent educational and team‑bonding tool**. We recommend deploying it as a free, open‑source game and focusing on user growth rather than direct revenue. If adopted by a large organisation in the Philippines, the cost savings from improved remote collaboration can justify the development investment.
